# HR Analytics: Employee Attrition & Workforce Insights Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify key workforce trends and factors influencing employee turnover. The dashboard provides HR teams with actionable insights to improve employee retention and support data-driven workforce planning.

---

## Business Problem

Employee attrition can increase hiring costs, reduce productivity, and impact organizational performance. This project helps answer questions such as:

- Which department has the highest attrition?
- Does overtime increase employee attrition?
- Which job roles are at higher risk?
- How do salary and experience influence attrition?
- What actions can improve employee retention?

## Key KPIs

- Total Employees
- Active Employees
- Employees Left
- Attrition Rate
- Average Monthly Salary
- Average Years at Company

---

## Business Insights

- Identified employee attrition trends across departments.
- Analyzed the impact of overtime on employee turnover.
- Evaluated attrition by job role, salary, and experience.
- Provided HR recommendations to improve employee retention.
